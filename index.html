Our Story

Beautiful things, made responsibly.

ECOSAK began with a simple idea: that a bag could be vibrant, durable, and kind to the planet all at once. Each tote is handwoven from recycled plastic by artisans who turn discarded material into something worth carrying for years.

No two are exactly alike. The colors, the weave, the small variations — they're the signature of a human hand, not a machine.
